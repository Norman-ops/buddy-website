About Us

Purpose of CTU-Buddy
The CTU-Buddy website is designed to be a comprehensive companion for students at CTU, providing them with essential resources, tools, and support throughout their academic journey. It aims to streamline the student experience by offering easy access to important information, academic assistance, and community engagement, all in one convenient platform. CTU-Buddy is built to enhance learning, foster collaboration, and ensure that students are well-equipped to succeed in their studies and beyond.

Mission of CTU-Buddy
Our mission is to empower students at CTU with the resources and support they need to achieve academic excellence and personal growth. We are committed to creating a user-friendly platform that bridges the gap between students and the various services available at CTU. By fostering a sense of community and providing tailored assistance, CTU-Buddy strives to make the student experience more enriching, engaging, and successful.

Meet the Team
CTU-Buddy is brought to life by a dedicated team of passionate individuals who are committed to enhancing the student experience. The team responsible for its development includes Gundo, Thama, and Bonginkosi. Together, they have combined their skills and expertise to create a platform that truly meets the needs of CTU students.
